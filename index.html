How to Play

  • Each player takes turns rolling the dice.
  • Your piece moves forward by the number you roll.
  • If you land on a star, you jump ahead to a higher tile.
  • If you land on a black hole, you fall back to a lower tile.
  • Tile 99 is always a black hole that sends you back to the start!
  • The first player to reach tile 100 wins.
  • Good luck and have fun!
